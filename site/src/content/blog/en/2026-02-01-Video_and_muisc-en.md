If you’re still on VLC’s dated UI, these **modern open-source players** offer the same strong cores (LibVLC or MPV) with 2026-style UIs: rounded corners, Mica/Acrylic, dark mode, minimal layout.

## By platform

- **Windows**: Screenbox (Fluent + Mica, LibVLC), SMPlayer (Qt, MPV)
- **Linux**: Celluloid (GTK4/Libadwaita), SMPlayer
- **macOS**: IINA (native, MPV)

All are ad-free and format-capable. For install commands and tuning, see the [Chinese version](2026-02-01-Video_and_muisc) of this post.
