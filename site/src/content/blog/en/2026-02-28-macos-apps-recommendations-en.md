A short list of apps I find useful on macOS (including Hackintosh), by category: browser, player, clipboard, password manager, uninstall, system maintenance, screen recording, download. Updated as I use more.

## Browser: Floorp

**Floorp** is a free, open-source browser based on **Firefox** and the **Gecko** engine. It keeps Firefox’s privacy and customization while adding workspaces, sidebars, vertical tabs, and workflow tweaks. [floorp.app](https://floorp.app/) (MPL 2.0).

## Media: IINA, etc.

- **IINA**: Native macOS player, MPV-based, great for local video.
- **Motrix**: Download manager (aria2), supports BT and HTTP(S).

## Utilities

- **OnyX**: System maintenance and tuning (caches, logs, permissions).
- **Kap**: Simple screen recording (GIF/video), open source.
- **Maccy**: Clipboard history.
- **KeePassXC**: Password manager, cross-platform.
- **AppCleaner**: Uninstall apps and remove leftovers.
